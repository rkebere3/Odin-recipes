Shirowot

Description

Shirowot, also known simply as "shiro," is a flavorful and hearty stew that is a staple in Ethiopian cuisine. It is a thick, spicy dish made primarily from ground chickpeas or lentils (though other legumes such as split peas can also be used), seasoned with a blend of spices and often cooked with onions, garlic, and tomatoes. The dish typically has a smooth texture, although it can vary depending on personal preferences and regional variations.

  • 1 cup of Chickpea flour

  3. Stir in the tomatoes and mix.
